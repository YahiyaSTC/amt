HOW WE GOT HERE!

Companies worldwide have been facing an acute talent crunch, and finding the right talent in their location has become challenging. On the other hand, geographical boundaries prove a barrier for talented professionals deserving better prospects and pay.

This points towards the need for an ecosystem that connects the right opportunities with the right talent. AMT is turning the aspiration of meritorious talent by connecting them with global opportunities while making it easy for companies across the globe to find the right talent.
