ABOUT US

AMT Staffing is a supplemental staffing agency specializing in the medical, dental, private duty and travel fields of healthcare staffing and employment. AMT Staffing has a long history and proven record of bringing qualified healthcare professionals together with hospitals, healthcare providers and other entities for their short and long-term needs. AMT Staffing is skilled in high volume, just in time recruiting and staffing as well as detailed, specific searches for long-term needs. We achieve this capability through integrating technology, efficiency and qualified, experienced staff who are personally invested in positive outcomes for our team members and our clients.
